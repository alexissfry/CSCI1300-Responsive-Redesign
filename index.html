About Us

Pastiche is an intimate, European-style patisserie founded by Eileen Collins and Brandt Heckert in the heart of the historic Federal Hill. Pastiche originally started as a fledgling business providing up-and-coming restaurants with our homemade desserts. Since opening our shop in 1983, we have been known for our classic desserts that are handmade in-house. For the past four decades, our approach has always been the same: offer the best quality and deliver it consistently.

We invite you to come enjoy an afternoon cup of tea or a cappuccino and a delicious dessert!
